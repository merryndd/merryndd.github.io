Research profile

My work is in sociolinguistics, with particular focus on the southern Brythonic Celtic languages (Cornish and Breton), although I have also worked on Romance languages. My research interests include language ideology, language standardisation, languages in the media (particularly new media), language and identity, orthography, and lexical variation. For my current PhD I am investigating the lexicon of Breton in media domains and how this intersects with stereotypes about ‘new speakers’ of the language.
