Cookies

This site stores one cookie in order to remember your language preference. It does not track any specific data on users. Nonetheless, the law requires that users be informed of this and be given the chance to opt out. To read how to opt out of cookie storage, click this link.

Any other cookies stored by this site are (ironically) a result of implementing the cookie pop-up.

Ma udn cookie et an gwiasva ma, rag perthy cov a’n tavas a wruga whei dewis. Nag ujy an gwiasva o qwitha kedhlow ’dro dhe’n dus a ujy mires orto, mes herwydh an lagha, res ew dhebm lawl hebma dhe whei ha gara whei dhe refusya. Mar mennowgh gwul hedna, whei ell redya fatel y wul obma.

Mars era whei o qweles moy vel udn cookie, hedn ew awos an cookies erel dhe voas sewyans comprehendyans an gist a ujy ’gas kedhla ’dro dhe’n cookies. Nag eroma o qwul ges!
