Other documents

Scrifow erel h.e.

In 2019 I collaborated with Prof. Ken MacKinnon and edited a collection of his papers relating to Cornish and Cornwall, which you can view here.

En 2019 a wrugav vy gonis gen Prof. Ken MacKinnon rag parujy cuntel a scrifow scrifes ganjo ’dro dhe Gernowek ha Kernow; whei a ell y weles obma.

My MA thesis (University of York, 2016) discussed the Cornish orthography debate and the SWF with a focus on the role of ideologies.

Whei a ell redya an dornscrif MA vy (pednscol Evrok, 2016) a ujy clappya ’dro dhe’n dadhlow en-kever an letherednans kernowek hag an FSS.
