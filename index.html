Dr Merryn Davies-Deacon

Welcome to my website, which I mostly use for sharing resources relating to Cornish (and a few relating to Breton), accompanied by an occasional blog.

I’m currently employed as a lecturer in French linguistics at Queen’s University Belfast.

Theroma o usya an wiasva ma en broassa radn rag gwitha asnodhow en-kever Kernowek (ha nebes rag Bretonek ewedh).

Ma blog keffres, lebma eroma o scrifa (en maner terweythyek) ’dro dhe tacklow kelmes.

Theroma o conis an vledhen ma vel arethores a’n ethonieth Frynkek ort pednscol an Vyghternes, Béal Feirste.
