Merryn Davies-Deacon

I’m Merryn, a third-year PhD student at Queen’s University, Belfast, where I’m working on lexical variation in Breton and its relation to ‘new speaker’ identity as a member of the MEITS project.

More generally, my work is usually on Cornish and/or Breton from a sociolinguistic perspective, focusing specifically on ideology and language standardisation, languages in the media, identity, and lexical variation. Click here to view my full CV, including links to my research outputs. Click here for a list of conference presentations I’ve given, with details of abstracts, slides, references, etc.

On this site I also list a few resources in the domains of Cornish and Breton that I’ve created or compiled, and very occasionally blog about my research too.

If you’d like to get in touch, please do so via email or Twitter. You can also follow me on academia.edu or view my institutionally-sanctioned PURE profile.

Ow hanow vy ew Merryn ha theroma o conis war PhD, lebmyn et y dryja bledhen, ort Pednscol an Vyghternes, Béal Feirste. An testen a’m studhyans ew an varyanjow en-mesk an geryow en Bretonek hag an kelm tredh hebma ha honanieth an “gowsoryon nowydh”. Theroma o qwul an gonis ma vel radn a v/MEITS.

War nivel moy ledan, theroma o conis gen Kernowek ha Bretonek hag an ethonieth-socyologieth. Broassa radn an termyn erom o corra poos war an tybyanjow senjys war an tavajow, an gwreans a furvow standard a’n tavajow, an tavajow et an media, an honanieth, hag an varyanjow en-mesk an geryow. Whei a ell gweles ow CV vy ettien obma, gen gorgevrednow a ujy o moas dhe’n scrifow vy ha dhe asnodhow kelmys erel. Whei a ell gweles rol an cowsow reies genev-vy obma, gen an berrscrifow, an imajys towlys, an scrifow a wrugav gwul mencyon anedhans, h.e.

Ma an gwiasva ma o comprehendya ewedh gorgevrednow troha nebes asnodhow rag Kernowek ha Bretonek, nebes anedhans gwres genev vy, hag ow blog academek neb ew nowedhys der vaner terweythus.

Mars eus whans dhe whei clappya gena vy, whei a ell y wul dredh ebost po Twitter. Whei a ell ow sewya dredh academia.edu po gweles ow folenn PURE vy ort an gwiasva a’m bednscol.
