Merryn Davies-Deacon

Hi, I’m Merryn, a second-year PhD student at Queen’s University, Belfast, where I’m working on lexical variation in Breton and its relation to ‘new speaker’ identity as a member of the MEITS project.

My research interests are in sociolinguistics, specifically ideology and language standardisation, languages in the media, identity, and lexical variation. To find out more about my research, click here.

On this site I also list some resources that may be of interest to anyone wishing to learn more about certain aspects of my research areas.
