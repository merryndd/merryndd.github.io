Resources

Cornish

The form of Cornish I grew up with, and am most comfortable with, is usually known by its speakers as Modern Cornish or Kernôwek Bew. I’ve compiled a few audio examples of this variety in this folder. Elsewhere on the internet, you can find some basic information about Modern Cornish here. For learners, I recommend Jan Lobb’s blog (beginner–intermediate) and Neil Kennedy’s blog (intermediate–advanced). There’s also Dan Prohaska’s audio course for beginners.

I’ve also converted Nicholas Williams’ Clappya Kernowek into a Memrise course, although this is a different variety of Cornish and the orthography is now outdated.

On the subject of orthographies, one of my most audacious experiments with Microsoft Excel to date is a spreadsheet that converts between various forms of the Standard Written Form of Cornish (and also outputs mutation tables). Click here to download the latest version.

Breton

I’ve created a few Memrise courses for Breton based on existing materials: Brezhoneg … buan hag aes (for English speakers) and syntax examples from Hentenn Oulpan 1 (for French speakers), as well as some of the technical vocabulary lists created by Kreizenn ar geriaouenn. In addition to this I have a long list of links to online resources, mostly passed on by helpful Breton teachers, that I hope to sort through and make public one day …

Asnodhow

Kernowek

My a wrug tevy gen “Kernowek Modern”, neb ew creies “Kernôwek Bew” terweythyow hedhyw. Therom o usya an furv ma termyn erom o clappya Kernowek e’n dedhyow ma: termyn erom o scrifa, therom ow usya an FSS (furv gen grafys “diwedhes” ha “hengovek”).

Rag an dus a eus whans ganjans desky moy ’dro dhe’n furv Kernowek ma, my a wrug gorra nebes sompelys a’n tavas cowsys et an le ma. En leow erel a’n kesrosweyth, whei a ell cawas kedhlow elvednek ’dro dhe Kernowek Modern obma (en Sowsnek). Mars eus whans dhe whei desky, therom o comendya blog Jan Lobb (rag an dhallethoryon) ha blog Neil Kennedy (rag an dus moy avonsys). Whei a ell goslowes ort steus Dan Prohaska ewedh, neb ew war anow.

My a wrug treylya an lever Clappya Kernowek gen Nicholas Williams en steus Memrise ewedh, bes ma va o usya furv aral a Gernowek (Kernowek Unys Amendys) hag nag ew hebma usyes gen meur a dus na moy.

Nanj ew nebes misyow a wrugav vy gwul restren Microsoft Excel a ell chanjya tredh furvow dihaval an FSS hag a ell rei dhe whei treylyanjow an kessonednow kensa ewedh. Whei a ell iscarga an versyon an moy nowydh obma.

Bretonek

My a wrug gwul nebes steusow war Memrise rag Bretonek neb ew fondyes war anodhow a wrug doas dhe voas dyllys e’n termyn eus passyes: Brezhoneg … buan hag aes (rag an Sowsnegoryon) ha nebes esompelys a fatel araya an lavarow tednys dhort Hentenn Oulpan 1 (rag an Frenkegoryon), hag ewedh nebes rolyow gen geryow moy complek gwres gen Kreizenn ar geriaouenn. Ma genev vy ewedh rol hir a asnodhow warlinen reies dhebm gen descajoryon, ha ma govenek dhebm my dhe sortya oll a hebma hag y gorra war an gwiasva ma neb jorna e’n termyn a dheu …
