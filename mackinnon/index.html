Papers on Cornwall and the Cornish language, by Ken MacKinnon

In 2019 I worked with Prof. Ken MacKinnon (1933–2021) to make a number of his conference papers and other writings on Cornish and Cornwall available online. You can download the booklet, containing ten items spanning the period 2002–2019, here:

Papers on Cornwall and the Cornish language, A5 size for reading onscreen, 299 pp.

Papers on Cornwall and the Cornish language, on A4 paper for printing and assembling as a booklet, 75 sheets.

Scrifow en-kever Kernow ha Kernowek, gen Ken MacKinnon

En 2019, my a veu o conis gen Prof. Ken MacKinnon (1933–2021), ha nei a wrug parujy nebes cowsow dhort kescussulyanjow ha taklow erel a wrug ev screfa rag ens gwul es dhe gawas warlinen. Ma deg scryf et an levren, screfys tredh 2002 ha 2019; whei a ell hy iscarga obma:

Papers on Cornwall and the Cornish language, war paper A5 a ell boas redyes dre scrin, 299 f.
